Handling Of Storage

FortOilStorage provides comprehensive storage services in Singapore and at strategic locations worldwide, including the Netherlands, Fujairah, and Houston. Our facilities are designed to meet the precise requirements of our customers, featuring seamless connections for barges, ships, trains, and trucks, flexible operating hours, and efficient loading and unloading capabilities. Safety is at the core of every operation, and our team ensures that every product stored is handled with the utmost care, delivering reliable and high-quality service at all times.

Our tank storage network offers a wide range of capacities and configurations, allowing us to handle diverse products such as fuel oil, middle distillates, edible and vegetable oils, base oils, oleochemicals, non-hazardous chemicals, biodiesel, liquid agricultural products, and other petroleum products. With modern infrastructure and strategic locations, FortOilStorage provides customers with integrated storage solutions that support global logistics, streamline supply chains, and maintain the highest standards of safety and operational excellence.

Our enhanced scale combined with high-quality assets, low breakeven pricing, and experienced leadership make us a premier operator in the region.
