About Us

Offering Robust Tank Storage

FortOilStorage operates as a fully integrated tank farm and logistics provider, specializing in the safe storage and efficient movement of petroleum and chemical products. With strategically located terminals in Singapore, the Netherlands, Fujairah, and Houston, we manage the entire logistics chain for our customers with precision and reliability.

Our facilities collectively provide a storage capacity of over 8.1 million cubic metres, enabling us to handle a wide range of liquid fuel products, including crude oil, gasoline, petrochemicals, base oils, Virgin D6 fuel oil, and jet fuel. These terminals are strategically positioned at major global ports, offering seamless access to key shipping routes and refining hubs.

OUR STRATEGIC APPROACH

At FortOilStorage, we invest strategically in expanding our storage and handling capabilities, not only for petroleum products but also for agri and mineral liquids, alongside processing activities within our liquid bulk portfolio. Our growth is supported through greenfield developments, acquisitions of operational terminals, and enhancements at our existing locations in Singapore, the Netherlands, Fujairah, and Houston.

We actively pursue sustainable growth opportunities, focusing on energy transition initiatives such as hydrogen, chemical recycling, and chilled gases, while exploring adjacent commercial potential. By innovating responsibly, we ensure that our operations remain aligned with the evolving global energy landscape.

Corporate Governance

FortOilStorage operates with a two-tier board structure that clearly separates management from supervision. The Management Board is responsible for defining and implementing the company's long-term strategy while overseeing day-to-day operations. It is advised and reviewed by the Supervisory Board to ensure accountability and effective oversight.

Strong corporate governance is central to our operations. The Management Board upholds honesty and integrity in all business activities, while the Supervisory Board ensures efficient monitoring of performance and compliance with best practices.

This framework, built on a culture of integrity, professionalism, and respect, provides the foundation for FortOilStorage's commitment to transparency, stakeholder confidence, and sustainable business growth.

Our Purpose And Vision

At FortOilStorage, our goal is to be the connected partner for environmentally responsible storage solutions. We are committed to creating lasting value for our clients, communities, employees, subcontractors, and shareholders, ensuring our operations remain relevant and impactful.

We strive to deliver best-in-class service, sustainability, and efficiency, while maintaining a proactive 100% safety culture. As guardians of our customer's products, we ensure that all goods in our facilities are handled with the utmost care, protecting both people and the environment.

Through continuous investment in operational excellence and innovative terminal solutions, FortOilStorage enhances performance across our global network. Our focus on long-term environmental sustainability ensures that every decision contributes to safer, cleaner, and more efficient logistics operations.
